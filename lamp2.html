Carla Budar 2024

Rope Lamp

Rope, Resin, Concrete, Electronics (2021)

This project was a material exploration with concrete, resin, and other supporting elements. I was intrigued in how shape manipulation can vary based on the base material behaves.


Materials

Base:
Resin + concrete

Rope:
Twisted jute rope (fastened using resin)

Light:
Plastic tube + corn led bulb + parchment paper (diffuser)
